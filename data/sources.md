# Data Sources

This document lists primary data sources used as inputs to the
Influence Risk Engine.

All raw data is treated as input only; transformations and scoring
logic are handled explicitly in the model code.

## Baseline Structural Inputs (BIS)

- GDP (Nominal / PPP): World Bank, IMF, Maddison Project
- Population & Demographics: World Bank, UN DESA
- Military Capacity: SIPRI, IISS
- Trade & Chokepoints: World Bank, WTO
- Technology & R&D: OECD, WIPO

## Trend and Fragility Indicators

- Political polarization and governance indices
- Debt, fiscal stress, and capital flows
- Alliance cohesion and treaty participation
- Demographic and labor-force trends

Exact datasets and vintages will be recorded alongside each processed dataset.
